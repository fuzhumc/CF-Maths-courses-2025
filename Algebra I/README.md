# Algebra I

Here are the course materials for the algebra I. The teacher of this course is [yongqi LIANG](https://faculty.ustc.edu.cn/yqliang/index.jsp)

Alegebra I is a new course that designed for the freshmen of the China-France Mathematics Talents Class. The textbooks are selected from the original French textbooks. With the principle of step by step, the teaching will summarize and incorporate the classical types of analysis problems and demonstrate classified explanation. The corresponding exercises will help consolidate the foundation, inspire the ideas and cultivate the students'ability to analyze and solve problems. Examples and proofs are typical, flexible and innovative, aiming at cultivating and improving students'comprehensive mathematical thinking and skills.
