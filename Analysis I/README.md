# Analysis I

Here are the course materials for the analysis I  The teacher of this course is [Shibing Chen](https://faculty.ustc.edu.cn/chenshibing/zh_CN/index.htm).

Analysis I is a new course that designed for the freshmen of the China-France Mathematics Talents Class. The textbooks are selected from the French original textbooks. With the principle of step by step, the teaching will summarize and incorporate the classical types of analysis problems and demonstrate classified explanation. The corresponding exercises will consolidate the foundation, inspire the ideas and cultivate the students'ability to analyze and solve problems. Examples and proofs are typical, flexible and innovative, aiming at cultivating and improving students'comprehensive mathematical thinking and skills.
